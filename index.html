ABOUT ME

This portfolio showcases my work in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Remote Sensing, and Spatial Analysis, where technology and spatial thinking come together to solve real-world problems. My work combines geospatial data, satellite imagery, and analytical tools to transform complex spatial information into clear, meaningful insights I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Spatial Planning from Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology. Through my academic training and personal projects, I have developed skills in spatial data analysis, geospatial mapping, satellite image interpretation, and GIS-based decision support.

My approach to geospatial work combines technical analysis with practical problem solving. I believe that maps and spatial data should not only display information but also support informed planning, environmental monitoring, and sustainable development. From data collection and processing to visualization and interpretation, I focus on building spatial solutions that are both analytically sound and visually clear. My goal is to continuously expand my skills in GIS, Remote Sensing, and geospatial programming, using tools such as Python and modern GIS platforms to develop innovative spatial solutions. Through this portfolio, I aim to demonstrate my growing expertise and my passion for using geospatial technologies to better understand our world and support smarter decision-making..
